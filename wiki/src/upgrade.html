Choose your manual upgrade scenario

Upgrade from another Tails

If you know someone you trust who already did the upgrade, you can upgrade your Tails by cloning from it.

You need:

  • Your Tails USB stick
  • Another up-to-date Tails (USB stick or DVD)
  • ¼ hour to upgrade
